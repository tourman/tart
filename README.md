# tart
A decision maker, which is based on the [classic MADM approach](http://bit.ly/2vax7qZ).

# Get familiar
Every day we make different solutions intentionally or subconsciously.
Though a person, who has been diving deep into time-management tools for a certain time, should be familiar with an approach suggested even 200 years ago by B. Franklin.
Making a decision it's not so hard to write out all the pros and cons to two columns on a sheet of paper.
However, as soon as a time-developer try to represent the weight of every attribute,
he can encounter with an idea of visualization of those significances.
So this tool allows you to visualize all the pros and cons and, therefore, facilitate your decision making.
